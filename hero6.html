About Michael

Michael Gremillion has spent over two decades serving in various ministry and leadership roles. His work is characterized by a deep reverence for the classics, a love for the written word, and a commitment to helping others find stability in a rapidly shifting culture.

Currently residing in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains with his family, Michael divides his time between pastoral duties, writing his next book, and hosting community discussions on the intersection of faith and the liberal arts.
